About Eco–Globe

We are Eco-Globe. Team that is introducing eco-friendliness to people in a right and a simple way.

We want more people to know why renewable energy is good. Why ocean pollution is more serious than we think. We want people to know that C02 in the earth’s atmosphere must be reduced. We want more people to understand that word “Eco-Friendliness” is way more important to us, it’s the key to saving our world. Becoming eco-friendly isn’t really that hard. It simply means having a lifestyle that is better for the environment. Learning to become eco-friendly is even easier, but it is very important. Sometimes, small steps can make a big difference.

Renewable Energy

Renewable Energy is way more important than we think. Most commonly used renewable energy sources are Solar, Wind, Hydropower and Geothermal. Solar energy is already being used in homes. While new investors with “making energy from moving air” in their mind are showing up constantly.

Ocean Pollution

While leading pollutant entering the oceans is sewage, plastic is a serious issue as well. Over a 90% of plastics are not recycled. Corporate giants producing products made from plastic such as Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Starbucks, McDonald’s are already looking forward for producing and delivering goods in a way that respects oceans and this planet.

Fossil Fuels and Other Fuels

For industrialized nations like the US, modern life is unthinkable without fossil fuels. These fuels are causing Acid Rains, Climate changes, Air, Land and Water pollution and they are health-harming. The combustion process also creates nitrogen oxides that lead to the creation of smog and pneumonia.

How can you be eco friendly?

Being eco-friendly simply means having a lifestyle that are better for the environment. Learning to be more environmentally friendly is easier than you think. You don’t have to jump in by changing everything, starting small is more sustainable and it is a part of your normal life.
